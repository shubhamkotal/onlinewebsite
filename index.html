FAQ

What is stock trading?
To generate a profit, stock traders acquire and sell stock in publicly traded corporations available on stock exchange. Buying and selling stocks through the internet is referred to as online stock trading. You can quickly purchase and sell share stocks, mutual funds, bonds, and other securities using an online share trading account.
Trading vs Investing
Short-term buying and selling of stocks is referred to as trading. Example, day trading. Investing, on the other hand, entails purchasing and keeping shares for a longer period of time, such as days, months, or years, before selling them.
Start online trading in India in 2 steps:
1. Select a stockbroker: You can open a demat and trading account through a stockbroker. A trading account is used to place a buy or sell order in the stock market, whereas a demat account is used to keep the shares you purchase in a digital format. You can also deposit funds into your bank account from your trading account. In your trading account, you can see live market prices for stocks. You can choose a stock to learn more about it, including historical prices, charts, and other information. After you've completed your research, you can begin purchasing stocks and beginning your investment adventure.
